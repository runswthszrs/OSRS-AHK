  • 15 Mining
  • Brightness on second notch in game ( ? ? ? ? ) <? where the dash is
  • Exp drops set to right side, white color, largest text size, permanent duration ? (XP Button next to HP, not RL plugin)

Required Plugins:

  • Camera (Off)
  • Mouse Tooltips (On)
  • Menu Entry Swapper (On)
  • Entity Hider (On) ? Hides other players
  • Inventory Tags (On) ? with the settings below

Setup:

  • Location with 3 adjacent iron nodes (Ardougne/Alkharid/Mining Guild/Piscatoris)
  • All ores, gems, clue scrolls, and unidentified minerals must be tagged with inventory group 4
  • Designate the coordinates of all 3 iron nodes to do this you must hover your cursor over the center of the iron node and press:
    • First Rock: Control+Shift+1
    • Second Rock: Control+Shift+2
    • Third Rock: Control+Shift+3

Note: You will hear a beep upon confirming each Iron node if you have system sounds turned on
Note: The first and second inventory slot DO NOT GET DROPPED. This is designed so you can put your pickaxe / teleports in the inventory.
