Pre-Requirements:

  • Runelite
  • 38 Thieving
  • Any ?Master Farmer? (Ardougne/Draynor/Zeah)
  • Shift-Drop must be enabled
  • Brightness on second notch in game ( ? ? ? ? ) <? where the dash is
  • Exp drops set to right side, white color, largest text size, permanent duration ? (XP Button next to HP, not RL plugin)

Required Plugins:

  • Camera (Off)
  • Entity Hider (On) ? players hidden
  • Mouse Tooltips (On) ? with Interface tooltips on
  • Inventory Tags (On) ? With the below settings
  • NPC Indicators (On) ? with with the ?highlight color? set to #00FFFF and ?Highlight Menu Names? off

 

Setup:

  • Whatever food you want marked with group 2 inventory tag ? As shown below
  • Mark the seeds you wish to drop, and make sure the last inventory space is free

Recommended:

  • 70+ Thieving
  • Start the script with the ?Master Farmer? close by (On-screen)
  • Avoid AFKing for long periods of time, as you will have to intervene to bank seeds manually.
