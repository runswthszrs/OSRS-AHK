How to use:

Enter the nightmare zone with a similar setup shown in the picture above (Use whatever ratio of absorbs/overloads as you want)
Quickly use the Rock Cake until 51HP, then use overload, then use a complete Absorbtion Potion, then activate the script.

Required:

RUNELITE
Rock Cake (In top left/first inventory spot)
Absorption Potions
?Dominics Coffer? must be marked with a Purple ?Ground Marker? as shown in the picture above. (Shift+Right click to place marker)

Plugin Requirements:

?Timers? {On}
?Nightmare Zone? {On} with the below settings.
Note: You can change ?Absorption Threshold? to whatever threshold you want the script to use an absorption dose at.

Any plugins that obstruct the NMZ plugin in the ?top left? potion of the screen must be disabled, such as:
Opponent Information {Off}
Boosts Information {Off}
Ground Marker (On) with the following settings. 

 

Recommended:
Overloads.
Standing in the corner of NMZ against melee-only creatures, so you don?t lose as many absorbtion hitpoints. (So only 2 creatures can attack you)

Tip: You can place ground markers holding shift and right clicking.
