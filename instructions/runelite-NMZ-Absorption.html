Pre-Requirements:

  • Runelite
  • Rock Cake
  • Absorption Potions
  • Overload Potions
  • Quick-Prayer must be set to ?Rapid Heal?
  • Brightness on second notch in game ( ? ? ? ? ) <? where the dash is

Required Plugins:

  • Interface Styles (Off)
  • Nightmare Zone (On) ? with ?Colour Below Threshold? set to #00FFFF (As per picture below)
  • Regeneration Meter (On) ? with the settings below (Disable this plugin if you?re a pure account with less than 17 prayer and wish to rely on the rock-cake to stay at 1hp.)
  • Ground Marker (On) ? with the settings below
  • Timers (On) ? with default settings (verify overload timer is on)
  • Mark ?Dominics Coffer? with a Purple ?Ground Marker? plugin as shown in the picture below (Shift + Right Click the coffer)
  • Any plugins that obstruct the NMZ plugin in the ?top left? portion of the screen must be disabled (Opponent Information, Boosts Information, Slayer)

Note: You can change ?Absorption Threshold? to whatever threshold you want the script to drink absorption doses. We recommend higher than 250 and below 750.

How to setup your dream:

  • Setup your dream as ?Hard Rumble Customizable?
  • Choose your bosses and gather your potions from the barrels
  • Enter your dream then drink 2-3 full absorption potions, run to a corner, drink 1 dose of overload and wait for your HP to drop, rock cake to 1HP, and then start the script

Note: Use whatever ratio of absorbs/overloads as you want, tips in discord Money-n-exp-channel pinned messages.
Tip: Stand in the corner of NMZ against melee-only creatures, so you don?t lose as many absorption points (Boss recommendations located in discord NMZ channels).

Potential Bugs:

  • Script will stop working if you zoom out to far and there is black showing on your scream inside of the dream
  • Script will stop working if there is anything blocking the potions above. Turn off plugins, and if there is a large pot that refuses to go away, completely reset your runelite settings
