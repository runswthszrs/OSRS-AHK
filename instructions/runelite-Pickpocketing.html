Knights of Ardougne bank instructions:

Setup:

  • Find a world with the knight of ardougne trapped in the bank (World 2 usually is trapped)
  • Empty Jugs marked with ?Inventory Tag 6 (Cyan)? (Optional, for dropping empty jugs)
  • Shift-click to drop must be enabled in the Runescape settings.
  • Change ?Highlight Style? to ?Tile? AND ?South west tile? in Npc Indicators
  • Place a green (#00FF00) tile marker on the bank, and put your food of choice in first bank spot (top left slot)
  • Go up the ladder in the bank, and place a yellow (#ffff00) object marker on the ladder (In case of accidental click on the ladder)
  • Individually Tag the one Knight of Ardougne in the bank (Shift+Right Click)
