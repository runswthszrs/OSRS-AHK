Bank Setup

Please read the appropriate instructions for the type of bar you wish to create

 

Bronze Bars

Copper and Tin ore in first 2 bank spots, with withdraw “X” preset to “14”
Make sure “X” is also selected for “Quantity”

Iron Bars/Silver Bars/Gold Bars

Iron/Silver/Gold ore in first bank spot
Make sure “All” is also selected for “Quantity”

Steel Bars

Iron ore in first bank spot
Coal in second bank spot
with withdraw “X” preset to “9”
Make sure “X” is also selected for “Quantity”

Mithril Bars

Mithril ore in first bank spot
Coal in second bank spot
with withdraw “X” preset to “5”
Make sure “X” is also selected for “Quantity”

Addy Bars

Addy ore in first bank spot
Coal in second bank spot
with withdraw “X” preset to “4”
Make sure “X” is also selected for “Quantity”

Rune Bars

Rune ore in first bank spot
Coal in second bank spot
with withdraw “X” preset to “3”
Make sure “X” is also selected for “Quantity”

Done!
