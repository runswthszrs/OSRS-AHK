Requirements:

Bluestacks
You must set the bank booth coordinates by hovering your cursor over a bank booth window and pressing Shift+F3 (You’ll hear a beep to confirm it) – The BlueStacks window MUST be active before doing this. – If this coordinate ever becomes inaccurate, you can re-set it again using Shift+F3 to asign the coordinate again.

Withdraw ‘X’ must be preset to ’14’
Bank Fillers Enabled (Required for the script to logout when out of supplies)

Jugs of water and Grapes must be in in the first two bank spots as shown in the picture above.
