Requirements:

Bluestacks
60 Smithing
Access to Blast Furnace
Coal Bag
Ice Gloves

How to Use:
Put Gold in the ‘Gold Coffer!’

Fully Zoom-Out

Must be on a Blast-Furnace world.

Coal Bag must be in your first inventory spot, and bank fillers must be used
to prevent you from depositing it.

Bank layout must match the below setup
1st = Coal (USE PLACEHOLDERS)
2nd = Iron/Mithril/Addy/Runeite (USE PLACEHOLDERS)
3rd-7th = Stamina Potions. (USE PLACEHOLDERS)

Note: If you’re making steel bars with a coal bag, place 27 coal on the conveyer belt before starting the script. 

Withdraw ‘X’ must be preset to:

Steel (With Coalbag) = Set to withdraw ‘All’
Steel (With  NO Coalbag) = 14
Mithril Script = 9
Adamantite Script = 12
Rune = Set to withdraw ‘All’

Experience drop duration must be changed to 1 second.
Exp Drop ‘Counter’ must be set to ‘Most recent’

5 Coordinates must be preset prior to starting the script, please refer to the below pictures!

Stand at bank chest and press CTRL+ALT+1 on bank chest

Stand at bank chest and press CTRL+ALT+2 on conveyer belt

Stand at conveyer belt and press CTRL+ALT+3 on conveyer belt

Stand at conveyer belt and press CTRL+ALT+4 on bar dispenser

Stand at bar dispenser and press CTRL+ALT+5 on bank chest

Stand here before starting the script.
