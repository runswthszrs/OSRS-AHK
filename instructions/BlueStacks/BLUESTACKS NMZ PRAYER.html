Requirements:
43 Prayer
Super/Divine Combat Potions (Optional)
Prayer Potions.
Click-to-drop DISABLED
All-Melee NMZ opponents.
How to Use(Steps):

  1. Enter Dream
  2. Enable Prayers of Choice (Protect From Melee + Anything Else you want) – DON’T USE QUICK PRAYERS, SET THEM MANUALLY.
  3. Start Script (Script will use a dose of combat potion on startup).

Note: You may have difficulties using your computer while this script is active, everytime the script uses a prayer potion/combat potion, you will lose control of your keyboard/window for a fraction of a second, this doesn’t happen THAT often, but might make doing other activities difficult (Like, playing an FPS (Shooter Game))
