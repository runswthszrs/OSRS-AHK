Requirements:
  • BlueStacks
  • Align your In-game zoom roughly half way through the zoom bar (Refer to top picture) so you can see both the chest and cooking range on-screen.
  • You must set both coordinates of the bank chest and cooking range pressing Shift+F3 and Shift+F4 (Refer to the above pictures – You’ll hear a beep to confirm them) – If these coordinates ever become inaccurate, just re-set them.
  • Bank Fillers Enabled (Required for the script to logout when out of supplies)
  • Raw Fish must be in first bank slot.
  • Bank Quantity must be set to ‘All’
