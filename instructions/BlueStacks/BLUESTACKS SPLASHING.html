Requirements:

  • BlueStacks
  • All spellbook filters must be enabled.
  • Atleast -65 magic attack (Full metal armour+vambraces)
  • Before starting, press Shift+F3 on the designated creature you want to splash on.
  • If you’re using a high-alch splashing script, place the item you want to high-alch it in your first inventory spot (top left)

Recommended:

  • It’s recommended to cast once manually yourself before assigning the Shift+F3 Coordinate, so that they’re stuck in the agressive position and won’t move.
