Tweaking BlueStacks is suggested as Jagex won’t be able to distinguish between your BlueStacks emulator versus a genuine phone.

PLEASE NOTE:
The Rooting process has already been completed on the VM. You ONLY need to do steps 11 – 13!

Prerequisite:
Download link for proper Bluestacks Version: BlueStacks 4.110.0.1081
Download Link for BSTweaker: BSTweaker.rar (Extract me!)

Text Guide:

Step 1. Open BSTweaker, and on the “Main” tab choose “Force Kill BS”
Step 2: Go to the “Root” tab on BSTweaker and click on the “Unlock” button
Step 3: Go to the “Main” tab and click “Start BS”
Step 4: Install “Root Checker” and “Device ID”  (Drag the files located inside the BSTweaker folder into BS)
Step 5: Click on the “Root” tab on the BSTweaker and click “Patch”
Step 6: Open the “Root Checker” app to verify you are rooted
Step 7: Click on the “Root” tab and “Install SuperSU”.
Step 8: Click on “Update Su Binary”, wait for BlueStacks to close, and then reopen it
Step 9: Open the “SuperSu” app, choose “Expert”. It will prompt to update SU binary, choose yes then “normal”. Click the “Home” picture in BlueStacks on bottom left.
Step 10: Open the “Root Checker” App, click “Verify Root” and grant access to the SuperSU app. If correctly done it should now state “This device now has root access!”
Step 11: Go to “Settings” on BSTweaker, choose the type of phone and country you want to emulate, Apply
Step 12: Go to “Config” Tab, Click “Read”, “Random”,  “Patch”, then “Apply”. Close BlueStacks.  (Force Close via main tab may be necessary)
Step 13: Open the “Settings” tab on BSTweaker, click on “Patch”, then “Random”, and then “Apply”. Then reopen BlueStacks

Optional Step: Using the “Device ID” App, check your created IMEI and Android ID match BSTweaker tool.
