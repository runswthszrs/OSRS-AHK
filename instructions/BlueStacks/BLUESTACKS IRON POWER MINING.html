If you’re using a dragon pickaxe, make sure it’s in your inventory, and not equipped! (Or the script will have issues)

Requirements:

BlueStacks
Fully-Zoomed in (In settings above brightness)
15 Mining
Click-to-drop ENABLED
Last 27 Inventory spaces free (For Iron Ores)
How to use:
Press CTRL+ALT 1, 2 and 3 in the center of each iron ore node to designate where they are located. (Fully Zoom in First!)

First Iron Ore = Ctrl+Alt+1
Second Iron Ore = Ctrl+Alt+2
Third Iron Ore = Ctrl+Alt+3
Then Start the script!

Note: You can alternatively use the script on 2 iron nodes instead of 3, in this scenario you have to press CTRL+ALT+1, on the first rock, and CTRL+ALT+2 and CTRL+ALT+3 on the second rock.
