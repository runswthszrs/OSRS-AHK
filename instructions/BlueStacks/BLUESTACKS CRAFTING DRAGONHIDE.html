Requirements:

Bluestacks
You must set the bank booth coordinates by hovering your cursor over a bank booth window and pressing Shift+F3 (You’ll hear a beep to confirm it) – If this coordinate ever becomes inaccurate, you can re-set it again using Shift+F3 to asign the coordinate again.

Bank withdraw quantity must be set to ‘All’
Bank Fillers Enabled (Required for the script to logout when out of supplies)

Dragonhides must be in in the first bank spot as shown in the picture above.

Needle and thread must be in your inventory, with bank fillers being used to prevent you from depositing them.
