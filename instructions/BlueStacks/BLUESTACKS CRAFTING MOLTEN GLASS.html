Requirements:

BlueStacks

  • Full Zoomed Out
  • You must set both coordinates of the furnace and bank booth prior to starting the script. (As per the above pictures) – You will hear a ‘Beep’ upon designating the coordinates for confirmation.
  • Bank Placeholders Enabled (Required for the script to logout when out of supplies)
  • Buckets of Sand & Soda Ash must be in the first two bank slots.
  • Bank Quantity must be set to ‘X’
  • Withdraw ‘X’ must be preset to ’14’
