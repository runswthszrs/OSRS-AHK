Requirements:

  • Bluestacks
    Set the bank booth coordinates by hovering your cursor over a bank booth window and pressing Shift+F3 (You’ll hear a beep to confirm it) – The BlueStacks window MUST be active before doing this. – If this coordinate ever becomes inaccurate, you can re-set it again using Shift+F3 to asign the coordinate again.
    Bank Fillers Enabled (Required for the script to logout when out of supplies)
