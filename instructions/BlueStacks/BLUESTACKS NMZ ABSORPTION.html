  • Overloads – (TOP PORTION OF INVENTORY)
  • Absorption – (BOTTOM PORTION OF INVENTORY)
  • Rock Cake – (LAST INVENTORY SPOT ON RIGHT)

How to setup:

  1. Enter Dream
  2. Drink 1 Overload
  3. Drink 1-3 Absorption
  4. Run to corner of choice
  5. Rock-cake yourself to 1 HP
  6. Start Script

Note: You may have difficulties using your computer while this script is active, every time the script uses a absorption/overload, you will lose control of your keyboard/window for a fraction of a second, it might make doing other activities difficult (Example of playing an FPS)
